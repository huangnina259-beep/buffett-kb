UHow Things Got This Way

’éĘ Interest rates that had been reduced to stimulate the economy. 
’éĘ Dissatisfaction with the resulting prospective returns on low-risk investments. 
’éĘ Inadequate risk aversion, and thus a willingness to step out on the risk curve in search

’éĘ A broad-scale willingness to try new things, such as structured products and
