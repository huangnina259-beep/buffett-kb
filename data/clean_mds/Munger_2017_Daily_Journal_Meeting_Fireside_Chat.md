APRIL 3, 2017 / RICHARD LEWIS / 19 COMMENTS 
Following the 2017 Daily Journal meeting, Charlie Munger treated everyone who stayed to an 
informal fireside chat.  For over two hours, he graciously answered any questions.  I 
transcribed this fireside chat verbatim and as accurately as possible. It was transcribed 
from this fantastic 1 hour and 48 minute recording of the talk.  Below is the transcript in its 
entirety.
